## What it is

A working product prototype and a 12-month roadmap built for a single company - MyGate, India's largest gated-community platform (5.7M households, 27,000 societies), which raised Rs 225 Cr in 2026 to expand beyond society management into real estate. The page lives at [sagarahuja.co/mygate](/mygate/) and is the application artefact, not a CV attached to it.

## The setup

- A clickable React prototype of "MyGate Homes" rendered inside an iPhone bezel. The phone pins as you scroll and swaps screens - rental agreement, moving services, home loan, society parking, AI search - in step with the roadmap being read.
- The product thesis: MyGate should not out-inventory the portals. It should win on the one thing portals cannot fake - verified residents, verified addresses, and gate-issued visit passes. Verified owners only, zero brokerage, no broker spam.
- A 12-month roadmap in two parts. Part A brings real estate to the app across four quarters. Part B is four bets to grow the platform, including a bold bet on a domestic-help benefits and credit platform.
- A "Why me" section mapping the work to my record, and a closing ask.
- PostHog wired in to see who lands, what gets read, and whether the contact CTA is clicked.

## Built with

React and Vite for the prototype. Claude for the pair-programming. GitHub Pages for hosting. PostHog free tier for analytics.

## Why this format

A normal application has the company doing all the inference - reading a CV and guessing at fit. A prototype flips it. I do the work of imagining myself in the role, and I show them the product I would build and the roadmap I would run. The conversation that starts after is a product conversation, not an interview.
