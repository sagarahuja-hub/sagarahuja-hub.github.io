## Context

The Marketing team at Skillz managed LiveOps campaigns manually across multiple titles. Campaign creation, scheduling, targeting, and reporting were time-intensive, repetitive, and required heavy coordination between Marketing and Product/Tech. This capped how often campaigns could run and how personalized they could be.

## Approach

- Conceptualized an **Agentic AI-driven tool** to automate the end-to-end campaign lifecycle: briefing, content generation, targeting logic, scheduling, and post-campaign reporting.
- Positioned the tool as a **Marketing-facing product** with AI as the operational backbone, not a back-end engineering tool.
- Led product vision, requirements definition, and stakeholder alignment across Marketing, Engineering, and Data teams.

## Result

- **50% increase in Marketing team efficiency** for campaign execution workflows.
- Enabled higher campaign frequency and faster iteration on LiveOps strategies.
- Established a template for embedding agentic AI in operational product surfaces.

## Why this mattered

Agentic AI is only useful in operational contexts if a non-technical end user can drive it. Framing this as a Marketing product (not a tech tool) was the central design choice that made adoption real.
